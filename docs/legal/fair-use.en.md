# Rion Studio Fair Use Rules

Version and effective date: 2026-07-14

## Core rule

Rion Studio is intended for personal, human-supervised assistance. You must remain present, understand what the app is doing, and be able to stop it. The existence of a feature does not mean a game or service permits that feature.

## Required conduct

- Review and follow every target service's terms, game rules, automation policy, anti-cheat policy, community guidelines, and account rules.
- Use macros only for small assistive sequences while actively supervising the affected game windows.
- Stop a macro or session if it behaves unexpectedly, affects another player, or risks violating a rule.
- Keep control of your accounts, sessions, device, proxies, and exported data.

## Prohibited conduct

- Unattended botting, farming, continuous play, or account operation while you are absent.
- Bypassing, disabling, probing, or evading anti-cheat, bot detection, access controls, rate limits, regional restrictions, or enforcement systems.
- Exploiting bugs, duplicating assets, manipulating a game economy, impersonating others, or obtaining an unfair competitive advantage.
- Harassing users, disrupting services, overloading systems, scraping without permission, or accessing accounts or data without authorization.
- Using Rion Studio for unlawful activity or distributing configurations designed primarily for prohibited conduct.

## Enforcement and responsibility

Third parties decide whether activity violates their rules and can restrict an account regardless of these Rules. The Rion Studio project does not monitor play, approve individual configurations, or promise that supervised use is permitted. A material violation ends the license granted by the Terms of Use.
